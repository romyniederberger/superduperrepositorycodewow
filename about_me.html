About Romy Niederberger

I'm Romy Niederberger – born in Iceland and currently living in Reykjavík, where I’m surrounded by the wild beauty of this mystical island. I love the cool, crisp climate: the fresh ocean air, the ever-changing skies, and the magical light that transforms even winter into something extraordinary. In Reykjavík, I run Mythico – a small adoption center for mythic creatures. From Polar Phoenixes to Glacier Foxes, each being comes from Iceland’s untamed nature. Some appear only during storms, others glow beneath the northern lights or vanish into the mist. Caring for these creatures is my passion. At Mythico, fantasy becomes reality – and every adoption begins a new legend.

Romys Myths

Thunderhoof

Origin: Iceland

A powerful horse-like creature with thunderous hooves and a body that glows like lightning. It roams Iceland’s highlands and can summon storms.

Stormflash Raven

Origin: Iceland

A large raven with black feathers that flash silver in lightning. It soars through storms, its call bringing thunder. Known as the gods’ messenger and protector of travelers.
